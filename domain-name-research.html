Answers

  • A domain name is the name of your website. This is also where your users can turn to when trying to access your content.
  • Domain names are so important because without them, the internet will be unable to address it properly and your users will be lost in the sea that is the worldwide web without a lifeboat.
  • Your domain name has to be unique so that no two users can hold the same website name and no one wants to have the same name as an inappropriate webpage.
  • We register our domain names due to the fact that it is highly important to identify the IP address, so that URl's an allow our users to access our content.
