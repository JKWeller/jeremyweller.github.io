What is a web server and Why would you need one?

  • A web server is a technological masterpiece that takes incoming requests from end users and translate them back to the user. We need servers to store data and process our data.

Explain why getting a domain name is not enough to put your site up on the web

  • Getting a domain name surely is a huge accomplishment, since this is going to be the address that your consumers are furiously typing away to buy your product.
  • This however is not enough to launch your site. It needs a host to push it up with all your content.

What service does a website hosting company provide?

  • A hosting company provides space to a server.
  • They can also offer based on packages complete website setup and formatting so you dont have to.
  • A host can also help you manage your site on a professional level.
  • Lastly, I would hope they offer a 24/7 around the clock help desk.

What are the most important characteristics to look for in selecting a good web hosting company?

  • Reliability
  • Support
  • Bandwidth
  • Speed
  • Security
  • Technological features

Research companies that provide website hosting plans. On average, what is the price range (price per month) for hosting a website?

  • On average, a hosting company can charge anywhere from $2-$5 based on accountability and features offered. Keep in mind these are basic levels of packages.

What are the most important features for you when reviewing a hosting plan?
