How does a search engine work?
A search engine work by searching through tons and tons of data on the interweb to pick out certain keywords in your search.
Why do different search-engines give different ranking to websites for the same search phrase?
Search engine algorithms are all different and display search results differently from one another.
What is a <meta /> tag?
Meta tags are used in the head section used to describe page content.These meta tags can also help your SEO rankings.
What is the purpose for Keyword and Description meta tags?
These tags can affect your SEO rankings on search engine websites. Theyre comprised of many keywords and descriptions of the site.
Why might you include meta tags in your pages?
TO alert web search engines that you have content on the interweb.
Do meta tags make your pages more easily searchable or improve its ranking?
Yes. However, you have to ensure you are using the right keywords or descriptions or you wont get a foot in the door.
