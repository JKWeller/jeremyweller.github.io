What does Quality Assurance mean for web site development?

  • Quality Assurance for the website means that its been thoroughly combed through for any errors such as spelling, validation scans, etc. This process allows the end user to experience a website that is both functional serving its purpose and actually works.

Describe the two key principles of Quality Assurance: "Fit for Purpose" and "Right First Time."

  • Completing the task the first time, the right way is to hopefully ensure NO REWORK! No one likes rework. There are standardized processes when it comes to QA for a reason. Use them. Fit for purpose would be to ensure that the site and its content are structured towards what the goal or purpose is for the website.
